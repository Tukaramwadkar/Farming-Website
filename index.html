About FarmFresh

Supporting Organic Farmers Across India by connecting them with knowledge, resources, and fair markets. We believe in sustainable agriculture that benefits both farmers and consumers.

Founded in 2020, FarmFresh has been at the forefront of promoting organic farming practices across rural and urban India. Our mission is to create a sustainable ecosystem where farmers thrive, consumers get healthy produce, and the environment benefits.

  • Promote sustainable farming practices for healthier produce and better yields.
  • Provide comprehensive resources and education to farmers in rural and urban areas.
  • Connect consumers directly with organic farms and local markets.
  • Support farmers with market access, fair pricing, and community networks.

1. Sustainable Soil & Crop Management

Crop Rotation & Diversity

Rotating crops like legumes, cereals, and vegetables improves soil nutrients, breaks pest cycles, and boosts yields by up to 25%.

Cover Crops & Organic Amendments

Use cover crops (e.g., clover, mustard) and compost to protect soil, enhance organic matter by 30%, and reduce erosion.

Biofertilizers & Green Manure

Natural fertilizers help nutrient uptake, improve soil structure, and support long-term fertility without harmful chemicals.

üíß

2. Water & Resource Efficiency

Smart Irrigation

Drip irrigation, rainwater harvesting, and moisture sensors help conserve water by 40-60% and supply it precisely where and when crops need it.

Water-Smart Practices for Dry Areas

Dry farming techniques retain soil moisture and work excellently in regions where rainfall is low or unpredictable.

3. Smart & Precision Agriculture

Precision Tools

Use drones, GPS mapping, satellites, IoT soil sensors, and data analytics to monitor crop health, soil moisture, and pest threats in real time.

AI-Driven Advisory & Crop Monitoring

Apps and AI tools can suggest optimal times to irrigate, fertilise, or harvest, improving efficiency by 30% and cutting costs significantly.

4. Eco-Friendly Pest & Weed Control

Integrated Pest Management (IPM)

Combines crop diversity, natural predators, and minimal chemicals to control pests sustainably while protecting beneficial insects.

Biopesticides & Natural Pest Barriers

Neem oil, beneficial insects, and trap crops reduce reliance on synthetic pesticides by 70-80% while maintaining crop health.

5. Regenerative & Organic Practices

No-Till & Reduced Tillage

Helps preserve soil structure, lowers erosion by 50%, and increases carbon sequestration in soil, combating climate change.

Agroforestry

Integrating trees with crops improves biodiversity, soil health, and provides extra income sources like fruits, nuts, or timber.

6. Tech Tips & Innovation

Remote Sensing & Satellite Imagery

Spot hidden crop stress early and make targeted decisions about inputs, reducing waste and improving yields by 15-20%.

Blockchain Traceability

Helps farmers track produce from seed to sale, which builds consumer trust and can improve market access and premium pricing.

7. Practical Everyday Farming Tips

Soil Testing

Regularly test pH and nutrient levels to tailor fertiliser use, avoid over-application, and save 20-30% on input costs.

Mechanisation

Using seed drills, power tillers, harvesters, and other affordable machines speeds up work by 60% and cuts labour costs.

Diversify Crops

Growing a variety of crops spreads risk, improves income stability, and ensures food security throughout the year.
